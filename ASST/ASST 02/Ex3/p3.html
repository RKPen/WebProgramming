About

Founded in 1866, the American University of Beirut bases its educational philosophy , standards , and practices on the American liberal arts model of higher education. A teaching-centered reaserch university , AUB has around 800 instructional faculty and a student body of 8,000 students. The University encourages freedom of thought and expression and seeks to graduate men and women comitted to creative and critical thinking , life-long learning , personal integrity, civic responsability and leadership. AUB's motto is That They May Have Life And Have It More Abundantly
